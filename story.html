about our story

have fun.

We met for the first time
in our first year of high school
without knowing anything.

For three years,
we spent meaningful time
studying, laughing, and chatting together.

After 3 years of good time,
we took the college entrance exam
and went to different regions and universities.

We went to different universities,
went to the army,
and enjoyed our daily lives as usual.

Even though we are apart,
we keep in touch and worry about each other.

It's been three years since we've been separated,
so we're going to get together
and do a meaningful project.

We have a team name called "Life Friends."
We will carry out various meaningful projects
with our life friends.
